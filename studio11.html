Studio 11, 2025




The video incorporates the space of Studio 11 on the fifth floor of Parsons East, the site where the performance Please Take a Seat took place. It explores the potential of chairs, bodies, forms, tools, objects, and apparatuses as modular units that occupy or erase space.

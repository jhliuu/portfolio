Jayden Liu（劉峻豪／Chun-Hao Liu）(b. Taiwan) is a Brooklyn-based artist currently pursuing an MFA in Fine Arts at Parsons School of Design. With a background in theater, his practice draws from everyday routines, social dynamics, and the small frictions that often go unnoticed.

Working across sculpture and performance, Liu creates situations that highlight the invisible scripts behind daily life. He is interested in how tension, miscommunication, and quiet contradictions play out in shared spaces.
